M Soma is a training program for Kenyan high school graduates who want to become software developers. Our mission is to provide scholars from low income families with the skills and community support they need to become professional software developers.

 

We operate as a non profit to ensure that the brightest minds in Kenya get access to great opportunities.

 

Our Programs

June Bootcamp

An immersive and intense four week program targeted for beginners. The first week of the bootcamp lays the groundwork for being a good programmer. We will start by answering questions like "What is programming?", "Which programming language(s) should I learn first?". The camp will culminate in one big final project.

Courses overview

  • Introduction to Programming
  • Web development
  • Entrepreneurship and Ethics

Online Learning

Our online courses are designed to improve your skills with self-propelled learning and flexible organization. There are endless online tutorials and books about programming, but hardly do you ever find one that actually tells you where to start. Save your time, money and frustration by enrolling in our online courses. Everyone was new to programming at some point in their lives. We design our online courses assuming that you have zero or very little knowledge about programming, and we take the "complex" parts and break them down into smaller chunks that make it easier to absorb. We will be launching our Android app in December for users with low bandwidth mobile devices and slow internet coverage.

FEATURES

  • Offline accessibility
  • Recommendations and discovery
  • Self paced learning

Volunteer Opportunity

Our courses are taught by volunteer software developers who offer their time and talents to mentor our scholars. This ensures our scholars have access to potential role models in the field. We welcome anyone who wants to give back to the Kenyan tech community by overseeing students' learning at our bootcamps.
