ABOUT US

M Soma is a training program for Kenyan high school graduates who want to become software developers. Our mission is to provide scholars from low income families with the skills and community support they need to become professional software developers.

We operate as a non profit to ensure that the brightest minds in Kenya get access to great opportunities.

Our Initiatives

Bootcamp

Our bootcamp offers our scholars hands-on, project-based learning experiences that are designed to give beginners the skills and confidence they need to become digital creators

Learn More

PRIMARY SCHOOLS PROGRAM

We develop a year long curriculum that is designed so that teachers with little to no coding experience are able to teach coding fundamentals to their primary school students

Learn More

ONLINE LEARNING

Slow internet and low bandwidth devices often at times limit access to learning content online. Our mobile apps are designed to solve this problem by making content available offline
